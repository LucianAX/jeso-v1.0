Our mission is to empower students in higher education in Denmark to expand their knowledge, encourage entrepreneurship and to help companies to grow and improve.

Our vision is to make the Junior Enterprise concept well known in Denmark and to make it easier for students to join the economy after their graduation.

Our values
We believe in the power of the network. We identify new opportunities and seek new challenges. We prefer actions to words and goals to dreams. We value the internationality and the multiculturalism of our organisation.

Our goal is to Equip members with more knowledge than the studies can provide and with the practical experience to join the economy after their graduation. we want to Become a well known and appreciated organisation in and around Sonderborg by providing value-creating services to local businesses.

ABOUT US

We are JESO, which is the abbreviation of Junior Enterprise Sonderborg. We are a non-profit organisation initiated in October 2019 by nine motivated students from SDU Sonderborg. We are currently a Junior Initiative, which embraces the Junior Enterprise concept and aims at becoming part of the Junior Enterprise Europe network by the end of October 2020.
