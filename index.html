ABOUT US

We are JESO, which is the abbreviation of Junior Enterprise Sonderborg. We are a non-profit organisation initiated in October 2019 by nine motivated students from SDU Sonderborg. We are currently a Junior Initiative, which embraces the Junior Enterprise concept and aims at becoming part of the Junior Enterprise Europe network by the end of October 2020.
